About

Welcome to Michele’s Catering Ala Carte! Catering that is uniquely different than most Caterers. We start with the main entrée that you have selected from our menu offerings. Then, add as many of the Ala Carte side selections to the main entrée that you want. We will assist you in creating the perfect meal for your event and your budget! If you want a specific Entree that is not on our menu, we can probably do it, just let us know what you were hoping for on this special day, and we will work it out!
If saving money is a main goal and time is not an issue, we can help you save money by letting you help us prepare some of your meal. Let us know if you are interested in helping, and we will fill you in on the details. So, what about serving, you ask? Well, you also have choices with that. You can hire us to serve the food at your event, or you can hire your own crew, friends, family, etc. Pretty cool huh? Let us know how we can help!
Is the food good? Crazy good! Not only that, it’s also made with health in mind. For instance, I mill my own flour for baked items, and choose fresh and healthy ingredients for all my dishes, as if I was making them for my own family. A list of ingredients can be provided upon your request. Now hear this!!! We can also adapt to Gluten Free and Vegan.
Feel free to contact me Michele Walker with any questions. Just to let you know a little about me; I have been making healthy foods for special events for the past 22 years. I have 7 kids who played multiple sports, and typical concession stand food was not an option for them. So, I volunteered to run the concession stand if I could make healthy meals to sell instead. Our sporting events concession became legendary! It was from here that I was being asked if I would cater a student’s graduation party, weddings, baby showers, etc. We moved to the K.C. Area in 2016 and started helping our kids and their friends with events too. We have catered weddings, rehearsal dinners, parties, funerals, and conferences.
